## Method Lessons from This Branch

1. **Hybrid structures are stable** — The method initially assumed hybrids might resolve to single structures. Three domains confirm hybrid is legitimate stable form.

2. **Three domains is the magic number** — One domain (finance) was comparative. Two (finance + compute) was a pattern. Three (finance + compute + pandemic) demonstrates method-shaping.

3. **Infrastructure concentration is more explicit** — Physical constraints (chips, vaccine production) make concentration mechanism more visible than financial abstraction.

4. **Public good dimension matters** — Not all concentration phenomena are equally private. Public good dimensions affect political responses and moral claims.
