## Notes by scenario

### Wealth Concentration Structural Stress

- strongest contribution:
  - opens the branch as a structural rather than moral framing
- main weakness:
  - remains more umbrella than bounded case
- why `keep`:
  - it still functions well as a parent branch scenario
- next step:
  - keep as umbrella, but do not rely on it as the strongest evidentiary lead

### US Asset-Regime and Wealth Lock-In

- strongest contribution:
  - clearest bounded test of `design/rule conflict under volatility`
- main weakness:
  - may overfocus the branch toward tax design if treated as the whole story
- why `keep`:
  - strongest current proving-ground lead
- next step:
  - keep as lead bounded case while comparing against stronger transmission cases

### Private-Credit Resilience Squeeze

- strongest contribution:
  - opens the transmission side of the branch
- main weakness:
  - less downstream and borrower-facing than the India case
- why `revise`:
  - useful conceptually, but should now function more as a parent transmission framing than as the branch's strongest transmission case
- next step:
  - treat India as the stronger operational transmission lead and revise this case into a broader transmission umbrella if needed

### India Last-Mile Credit Squeeze

- strongest contribution:
  - concretizes the transmission side through downstream lender and borrower fragility
- main weakness:
  - still needs stronger linkage to concentrated wealth at the top of the chain
- why `keep`:
  - strongest current transmission competitor to the rule-conflict lead
- next step:
  - compare directly against the US asset-regime mismatch case in future maturity judgments

## Branch-level judgment

- the branch is clearly beyond `grounded` and now firmly `comparative`
- the branch remains most honestly `hybrid`
- the bounded lead is currently rule-conflict
- the strongest challenger is now downstream transmission

## What this matrix changed

- it made explicit that the broad private-credit transmission case is no longer the best transmission lead
- it created the branch's first explicit `revise` judgment inside the non-climate proving-ground
- it strengthened the case that the branch is sufficiently structured for Level 3 but not yet justified at Level 4
